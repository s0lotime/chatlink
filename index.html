Welcome to Chatlink!
Chatlink is a place where you can talk to your friends unfiltered and unblocked.
You don't need any special designation or room setup. Just type in a room name and you can talk in it!
Keep in mind that all chatrooms are public, so if you don't want the chance of someone else seeing your messages, try to use a hard-to-find chatroom name. Things like random letters and numbers, special characters, etc.
There really are infinite chatrooms but here are some good ones:
General Chat
Gaming Chat
Music Lounge
Study Group
Xbox Chat
PlayStation Chat
If you don't really like these groups, you can choose a chatroom name and jump right in with your classmates or your friends!
There are two types of chatrooms: Persistent and Disappearing
Persistent means the messages stay forever unless deleted by the sender.
Disappearing means the messages will eventually disappear over some time automatically.
